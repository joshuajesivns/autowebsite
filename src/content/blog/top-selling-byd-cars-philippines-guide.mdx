BYD didn't just have a good year in the Philippines — it had the best year any single brand has posted in recent memory. **26,122 units sold in 2025**, a **446% jump** from 2024, enough to make BYD the country's **#3 best-selling car brand**, behind only Toyota and Mitsubishi. Here's the real ranking: which BYD models are actually moving, why buyers are choosing them, and what's coming next. Ang datos, hindi hype.

## Key Takeaways

- BYD Philippines sold **26,122 units in 2025** — up **446%** from **4,780 units in 2024** — making it the **#3 best-selling brand** in the country.
- The **BYD Seagull** (a budget urban EV hatchback) was 2025's standout at **4,608 units**; the **Sealion 6 DM-i** (compact PHEV SUV) has been a top performer two years running; the **Atto 3** was 2024's top BEV at 1,062 units.
- BYD is **clearly the #1 Chinese brand** in PH — while MG, Chery, Foton, and GAC all shrank in 2025, BYD grew nearly fivefold.
- Distributor **ACMobility** grew the dealer network from **2 outlets (early 2024) to 79 (Feb 2026)** — a huge factor behind the growth.
- Newer entrants like the **BYD Seal 5 DM-i** (Dynamic and Premium trims) are expanding BYD's reach into the compact PHEV sedan segment.

## The Top-Selling BYD Models

### BYD Seagull — 2025's Standout (4,608 units)

A small, affordable **BEV hatchback**, the Seagull hit the exact price point PH's urban EV buyers have been waiting for. No other BYD model outsold it in 2025, and it's the clearest evidence that price — not just tech — is what moves volume in this market. We've covered [what it costs to own across every model year it's been sold here](/blog/byd-seagull-pms-guide) in a dedicated guide.

### BYD Sealion 6 DM-i — The Steady Winner

A compact **PHEV SUV** that topped BYD's overall lineup in 2024 (2,669 units) and remained a top performer in 2025. Its appeal: plug-in flexibility with none of the range anxiety of a pure EV, at a price that undercuts most hybrid SUV rivals.

### BYD Atto 3 — The BEV Pioneer

BYD's original volume BEV in PH, and 2024's top-selling pure electric at **1,062 units (~45% of all BYD BEV sales that year)**. It remains the nameplate most associated with BYD's early PH push, built around the LFP "Blade Battery."

### BYD Seal 5 DM-i — The Compact PHEV Sedan Pushing Into New Territory

Launched in PH in **November 2024** in **Dynamic and Premium** trims, the Seal 5 DM-i extends BYD's PHEV strategy from SUVs into the compact sedan segment — Civic/Corolla territory, but with a plug and over 1,000 km of combined range. It's too new to have a confirmed sales ranking yet, but it's a key piece of BYD's 2025–2026 lineup expansion. ## Why BYD Is Pulling Away From Every Other Chinese Brand

The contrast with the rest of the pack is stark. In 2025, while BYD grew **446%**, the other Chinese marques in PH went the opposite direction: **MG** dipped 3.3% (8,715 units), **Chery** dropped 43.5%, **Foton** fell 12.9%, and **GAC** slid 44.1%. BYD isn't just leading Chinese brands in the Philippines — it's the only one actually growing.

Three things explain the gap:

1. **Distribution muscle.** ACMobility (part of Ayala Corporation) scaled the dealer network from 2 to 79 outlets in about two years — most competitors haven't matched that pace of physical presence.
2. **Lineup breadth.** BYD sells both BEVs (Seagull, Atto 3, Dolphin) and PHEVs (Sealion 6, Seal 5, Shark 6), letting it hit buyers who want to go full-electric and buyers who still want a gas engine as backup.
3. **Pricing and promos.** 0% financing offers and subsidies of ₱200,000–220,000 on select models (Dolphin, Atto 3, Tang), plus price cuts on the Sealion 5 DM-i and Seagull in early 2026, kept BYD aggressively priced against both ICE and other EV rivals.

The Philippines' zero-tariff policy on EV imports, extended through 2028, is also a tailwind that specifically favors BEV-heavy brands like BYD.

## What's Next: New BYD Launches to Watch

BYD's PH lineup keeps expanding. Recent and upcoming additions include the **Shark 6 DMO** (a PHEV pickup), the **Sealion 5 DM-i**, the **eMax 9 DM-i**, the new **Sealion 7 AWD** (₱2.588M, launched mid-2026), and the **Atto 2**, previewed at the 2026 Manila International Auto Show with a launch still to be confirmed. Expect this ranking to shift as newer models — including the Seal 5 — build up a full year of sales data.

## Verdict

BYD's 2025 wasn't a fluke — it's the product of an aggressive dealer rollout, a lineup that covers both BEV and PHEV buyers, and pricing that keeps undercutting the competition. The **Seagull, Sealion 6 DM-i, and Atto 3** are the proven volume sellers so far; watch the **Seal 5 DM-i** and the incoming **Sealion 7** and **Atto 2** to see where BYD's next growth comes from.
