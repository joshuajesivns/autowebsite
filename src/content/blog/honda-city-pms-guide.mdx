The **Honda City** is the Vios's classic rival — a bit more refined, strong on resale, and powered by Honda's 1.5L i-VTEC. It's reliable and reasonable to maintain, with **one rule you can't ignore: the CVT fluid.** Here's the full PMS breakdown. This is the Honda City companion to our [complete Car PMS guide](/blog/car-pms-philippines-guide).

## Key Takeaways

- Runs the standard **5,000 km / 6-month** PMS cycle.
- A regular City PMS is about **₱5,000–7,000**; a major 40K–80K service is **₱10,000–15,000+**. *(Average casa prices, June 2026 — verify with your dealer.)*
- **Honda parts cost a little more than Toyota's** — but not dramatically.
- **CVT fluid = the non-negotiable.** Use genuine **HCF-2** and change it on schedule.
- **Timing chain, not belt**, and strong resale value.

## Honda City PMS Cost by Interval

| PMS stage | Interval | Casa cost (City) |
|---|---|---|
| First PMS | 1,000–5,000 km | ₱2,000–4,000 *(usually free labor)* |
| Standard PMS | 10,000–40,000 km | ₱5,000–7,000 |
| Major PMS | 40,000–80,000 km | ₱10,000–15,000+ |

Honda parts and labor land **slightly above** the Vios, pero kaunti lang. For an exact figure, use Honda's official **PM Calculator**. For what's actually done at each interval, see the [main PMS guide](/blog/car-pms-philippines-guide).

## Common Wear Items

- **CVT fluid (HCF-2)** — the single most important service on this car. Don't skip, don't substitute.
- **Brake pads, wipers, battery** — normal wear.
- **Aircon** — works hard in PH heat; expect periodic cleaning.
- **Pang-ilalim** — bushings, tie rods, and links wear on rough roads. Listen for `lagutok`, `kalog`, and `kagat`; full sound guide is in the [PMS pillar](/blog/car-pms-philippines-guide).

## Casa vs. Talyer & Parts

Under warranty → **Honda casa** (and insist on genuine HCF-2). Out of warranty → a trusted talyer is cheaper for general work, but still use the correct fluids. Deciding between this and the Toyota? Compare with the [Vios PMS guide](/blog/toyota-vios-pms-guide).

## Verdict

The City is a refined, reliable daily with great resale and reasonable upkeep. The only thing standing between you and a long, happy ownership is **respecting the CVT-fluid schedule** — do that, keep your records, and it'll reward you.
